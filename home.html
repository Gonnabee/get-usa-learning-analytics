Data and statistics note. The presentation report (02) and the live-data dashboard draw on the actual export (24 students · 750 notes · 947 photos) and validated statistics — for MANOVA, the p=2 exact F(4,84)=0.49, p=0.747 (non-significant); for city × topic, Cramér's V=0.56–0.71 (large); and for objective fit, χ²(4)=61.4, Cohen's w=0.38. Because the notes are non-independent and some cells are sparse, we read the effect sizes as the primary evidence. The interactive dashboard (01) is computed from the same actual export — the per-student breakdown reflects direct measurement by user_id in the CSV (16 of the 24 students recorded at places mapped to a learning objective), and the objective coverage uses the same primary-objective distribution as the report (unseen 32% · authority 9%).
